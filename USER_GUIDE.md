# Buzzer User Guide

Welcome to Buzzer! This guide will walk you through everything you need to know to use the app.

## What is Buzzer?

Buzzer is an app that lets you remotely ring devices in your group. It's perfect for:
- Finding your lost phone quickly
- Alerting family members
- Coordinating with friends
- Finding your device in a group setting

Think of it like having a "find my phone" button that works across your entire group!

---

## Installation

### Requirements
- iPhone with iOS 12 or later
- Internet connection (WiFi or cellular data)
- Modern browser (Safari on iPhone)

### How to Install on Your iPhone

1. **Open Safari** on your iPhone
2. **Visit the app URL** provided by your group admin
3. **Tap the Share button** (the square with an arrow at the bottom)
4. **Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"**
5. **Enter a name** for the app (or keep the default "Buzzer")
6. **Tap "Add"**

The app will now appear on your home screen like a regular app! You can tap it to open Buzzer anytime.

### Important: Keep the App Open

For your device to ring when someone rings it, **the Buzzer app must remain open on your device**.

This means:
- ‚úÖ The app can be in the background
- ‚úÖ Your screen can be locked
- ‚úÖ The app can be hidden behind other apps
- ‚ùå You cannot swipe the app away from the app switcher

**Tip:** Leave the app open on your phone like you would with a walkie-talkie!

## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. **Open the Buzzer app**
2. **Tap "Sign Up"** on the login screen
3. **Enter your details:**
   - **Username** - A name others in your group will see (e.g., "Sarah's iPhone")
   - **Email** - Your email address
   - **Password** - Create a strong password (remember this!)
4. **Tap "Create Account"**

You'll now be logged in and ready to go!

### Logging In

If you already have an account:
1. **Enter your username or email**
2. **Enter your password**
3. **Tap "Login"**

You'll be taken to your dashboard where you can see your group and devices.

### Understanding the Dashboard

The dashboard is your main screen. Here's what you'll see:

- **Your Device** - Shows your phone's name and status (online/offline)
- **Group Name** - The name of your group
- **Group Members** - List of devices in your group
- **Status Indicator** - A green dot means the device is online and ready to ring

## Group Management

### What is a Group?

A group is a collection of iPhones that can ring each other. For example:
- Family group (all family members' phones)
- Friend group (group of friends)
- Coworker group

### Creating a Group

1. **On the dashboard, tap "Create Group"**
2. **Enter a name** for your group (e.g., "Smith Family")
3. **Tap "Create"**

The app will generate a **Group Code** that you can share with others.

### Joining an Existing Group

If someone shared a group code with you:

1. **On the dashboard, tap "Join Group"**
2. **Enter the Group Code** (ask whoever created the group)
3. **Tap "Join"**

You're now part of the group! Your device will appear to other members.

### Finding Your Group Code

1. **Tap the group name** at the top of the dashboard
2. **Look for "Group Code"**
3. **Share this code** with others who want to join

**Security Tip:** Only share your group code with people you trust. Anyone with the code can join and ring your devices!

### Leaving a Group

1. **Tap the group name** at the top of the dashboard
2. **Tap "Leave Group"**
3. **Confirm** when asked

You'll be removed from the group and won't see other members anymore.

## Device Management

### What is Device Registration?

When you first open Buzzer, you'll see a **"Register Device"** button. This tells the group that your phone is ready to be rung.

### Registering Your Device

1. **Tap "Register Device"**
2. **Give your device a name** (e.g., "Sarah's iPhone", "Dad's Phone")
3. **Tap "Register"**

Your device will now appear in the group's device list!

### Device Status

Each device shows a status:
- **üü¢ Online** - Device is active and ready to ring
- **üî¥ Offline** - Device is not currently active (app is closed or no internet)

### Changing Your Device Name

1. **Tap on your device name** in the dashboard
2. **Edit the name** (e.g., from "My Device" to "My iPhone 14")
3. **Tap "Save"**

**Naming Tips:**
- Include the device type (iPhone 13, iPad, etc.)
- Include who it belongs to (Sarah's phone, Mom's phone)

## Ringing Devices

### How to Ring Another Device

1. **Find the device you want to ring** in the group list
2. **Tap "Ring"** next to that device
3. **Select a duration:**
   - **30 seconds** - Short alert
   - **1 minute** - Standard ring
   - **2 minutes** - Longer ring
   - **Continuous** - Keeps ringing until someone stops it
4. **Tap "Start Ringing"** to confirm

The device will start ringing immediately!

### What You'll Hear and See

When your device is being rung, you'll see:
- **A loud ringtone** plays automatically
- **The screen flashes** "Device Ringing!"
- **A "Stop Ringing" button** appears

The device will ring until:
- Someone taps "Stop Ringing"
- The selected duration ends
- The device loses internet connection

### Stopping a Ring

If YOUR device is ringing:
- **Tap "Stop Ringing"** on the screen

If ANOTHER device is ringing:
- **Tap the "Stop" button** that appears in the group list
- The device will stop ringing immediately

## Tips & Best Practices

### Keep Your App Open

The #1 reason devices don't ring is because the app was swiped away. To keep the app running:

1. **Open Buzzer** on your home screen
2. **Leave it open** (you can use other apps, it stays running in background)
3. **Don't swipe it away** in the app switcher

**Visual Check:** Look for the battery percentage at the top. If you see Buzzer in the status bar, it's running.

### Internet Connection

Buzzer needs internet to work. Make sure:
- ‚úÖ You're connected to WiFi or cellular data
- ‚úÖ You have a strong signal
- ‚úÖ Your internet is fast enough (even 3G works)

### Device Naming

Good device names help everyone find the right phone quickly:
- ‚úÖ "Sarah's iPhone 14"
- ‚úÖ "Dad's Work Phone"
- ‚úÖ "Kids iPad"
- ‚ùå "My Phone" (too vague)
- ‚ùå "Device 1" (confusing)

### Group Security

- **Only share your group code** with people you trust
- **Only add real group members** - each device should belong to a real person
- **Keep your password private** - don't share your login
- **Leave groups you no longer want to be in**

### Testing

When you first set up Buzzer:
1. **Register your device** in the group
2. **Ask a group member to ring your phone**
3. **Test that audio plays** (even on silent!)
4. **Leave the app open** and check it still works after 5 minutes

## Troubleshooting

### My Device Isn't Ringing

**Problem:** Someone tried to ring you but you didn't hear anything.

**Solutions:**
1. **Check the app is open** - Open Buzzer and make sure it's visible on your screen
2. **Check your internet** - Make sure WiFi is connected
3. **Check your volume** - Press the volume up button to increase volume
4. **Restart the app** - Close Buzzer completely and reopen it
5. **Restart your phone** - Sometimes this fixes connection issues

### My Device Shows "Offline"

**Problem:** Your device shows as offline to the group.

**Solutions:**
1. **Check your internet connection** - Make sure you have WiFi or cellular data
2. **Open the Buzzer app** - Sometimes it reconnects automatically
3. **Wait 30 seconds** - The app updates status every 30 seconds
4. **Restart the app** - Force close and reopen Buzzer
5. **Check if app was closed** - Did you accidentally swipe it away?

### I Can't Create an Account

**Problem:** Getting an error when trying to sign up.

**Solutions:**
1. **Check your email** - Make sure the email address is correct
2. **Check your password** - Passwords must be at least 8 characters
3. **Try a different username** - Your username might already exist
4. **Check your internet** - Make sure you're connected
5. **Refresh the page** - Reload the app and try again

### Audio Isn't Playing

**Problem:** The device rings but no sound comes out.

**Solutions:**
1. **Check your volume** - Press the volume up button on the side of your phone
2. **Check Silent mode** - Look for the Silent/Vibrate toggle on the side of your phone
3. **Test with another app** - Try playing music or a video to test audio
4. **Restart your phone** - Sometimes audio settings get stuck
5. **Reinstall the app** - Remove and reinstall Buzzer from your home screen

### I Can't Join a Group

**Problem:** Getting an error when trying to join with a group code.

**Solutions:**
1. **Check the group code** - Make sure you typed it exactly (it's case-sensitive)
2. **Check the code is still valid** - Ask if the group code has changed
3. **Check your account** - Make sure you're logged in to the right account
4. **Wait a moment** - Sometimes the group code takes a few seconds to activate
5. **Try again later** - The group owner might have temporarily disabled new members

## FAQ

**Q: Can I use Buzzer on Android?**
A: Currently, Buzzer is designed for iPhone. Android support may come in the future!

**Q: How many people can be in a group?**
A: As many as you want! There's no limit.

**Q: Will my device ring if the screen is locked?**
A: Yes! As long as the app is open (even in background), your device will ring. The volume will play even if your phone is on silent mode (this is a special setting for this app).

**Q: What happens to my group if the group owner leaves?**
A: Other members can still use the group. You'll be able to ring each other normally.

**Q: Is my data private?**
A: Yes! Buzzer only stores information you explicitly share (username, group membership). We don't track your location or listen to your conversations.

**Q: Can someone ring my device without me adding them?**
A: No. Only members of your group (who joined with your group code) can ring you.

**Q: Will Buzzer drain my battery?**
A: Buzzer is designed to be battery-efficient. It uses less battery than most other apps.
